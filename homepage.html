Meet The Author

Mr. Walrus

"The time has come, the Walrus said, 'to talk of many things: of shoes, and ships, and sealing wax, of cabbage and kings. And why the sea is boiling hot and whether pigs have wings." -Lewis Carroll (Alice Through the Looking Glass)

Mr. Walrus is the lead editor for Sea Life Post and head of the North Atlantic Walrus Committee. He attended Bering Sea University were he majored in jounalism and computer science. He's also an avid blogger and epicure of seafood, particularly molluscs cucumber soup!
